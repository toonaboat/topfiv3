The high-gloss grey finish looks sleek, and the unit feels sturdy without bulk. The nonstick ceramic-coated basket accommodates family meals comfortably, fitting up to 3 lbs of wings or fries without crowding.

Performance

Cleaning

The dishwasher-safe basket and crisper plate make cleanup effortless. The nonstick coating is highly reliable, with minimal scrubbing required and no hard-to-reach crevices.

Ease of Use

Simple controls, clear display, and intuitive presets make operation smooth—even for beginner cooks. Preheating takes just a couple minutes, and the smart pause feature stops cooking instantly when the basket is removed.

Cooking Experience
