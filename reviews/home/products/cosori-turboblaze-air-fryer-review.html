COSORI TurboBlaze 6‑Qt Air Fryer Review
← Back to Air Fryers Rankings

When it comes to kitchen appliances that truly change the way you cook, the COSORI 6‑Quart TurboBlaze Air Fryer stands out as a serious contender. It's more than just an air fryer—it's a versatile, whisper-quiet kitchen companion that makes cooking healthier, faster, and a lot more fun.

Design & Build Quality

Right out of the box, this air fryer looks modern and sleek. The angled digital touchscreen is clear and intuitive, making it easy to operate, even for those new to air frying. It doesn't feel bulky on the countertop, despite offering a generous 6‑quart capacity—enough to cook for 2 to 4 people comfortably.

Unlike other models that blast hot air like a small jet engine, this COSORI runs quietly—even on Turbo mode. The fan noise is barely noticeable, making it ideal for open‑concept kitchens or small spaces.

Another notable plus: it stays cool underneath. Many air fryers heat up your countertops or require heat mats—this one doesn't. It's clearly designed with smarter airflow in mind.

Cooking Performance

Thanks to TurboBlaze Technology, the COSORI heats up quickly and maintains a consistent temperature between 90°F and 450°F. Whether you're roasting veggies, baking cookies, broiling steak, or dehydrating fruits, the results are impressively even.

Crispy chicken wings, golden fries, tender salmon—this machine does it all. The 9‑in‑1 functionality offers cooking modes like air fry, broil, roast, bake, reheat, dehydrate, proof, frozen, and even a customizable preheat option. The Turbo Modes take it up a notch, cutting down on cook time without sacrificing texture or moisture.

Bonus: the air fryer comes with a ceramic‑coated nonstick basket that's large enough to fit four chicken thighs or a whole batch of fries, and it's dishwasher‑safe for easy cleanup.

Ease of Use

The learning curve is minimal, which is perfect for beginners. The digital controls are well‑labeled, responsive, and allow mid‑cook adjustments—whether you're changing temperature or pausing to check doneness. It even resumes cooking automatically once the basket is placed back.

There's no built‑in shake reminder (something a few users noted), but most foods cook evenly without it. For those who like to monitor doneness mid‑cook, a quick pause and flip does the trick.

COSORI includes a high‑quality recipe book with over 30 chef‑curated recipes and a helpful reference guide, which makes experimenting with new dishes less intimidating.

Cleaning & Maintenance

Cleaning this unit is a breeze. The crisper plate stays in place when dumping food (a small but essential detail), and the basket's ceramic coating ensures nothing sticks. Both components are dishwasher safe, and the fryer's body easily wipes down with a damp cloth.

Who It's Best For: Busy parents who need fast, healthier meals; apartment dwellers with limited counter space; health-conscious cooks reducing oil; home chefs experimenting with dehydrating or proofing.
