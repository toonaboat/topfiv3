Typhur Dome 2 Air Fryer Review
← Back to Air Fryers Rankings

The Typhur Dome 2 redefines air frying with its unique dome design and groundbreaking self-cleaning technology. Built for serious home chefs who want restaurant-quality results with minimal maintenance, this innovative air fryer combines large capacity with smart app control.

Build Quality & Design

The distinctive dome design isn't just for show—it maximizes cooking space while ensuring optimal air circulation. The sleek stainless steel construction feels premium and durable, with a large transparent dome that lets you monitor cooking progress without interrupting the process.

Performance

With its powerful heating elements and precision temperature control ranging from 100°F to 400°F, the Dome 2 delivers consistent results across all cooking modes. The dome shape creates superior airflow patterns, resulting in more even cooking and better crisping than traditional basket-style fryers.

Self-Cleaning Technology

The game-changing self-cleaning cycle uses steam and high heat to break down grease and food residue. Simply add water, select the cleaning mode, and the unit handles the rest. This feature alone saves hours of manual scrubbing and extends the appliance's lifespan.

App Control & Smart Features

The Typhur app provides remote control, preset recipes, and cooking progress monitoring. Set timers, adjust temperatures, and receive notifications when your food is ready—all from your smartphone. The app also includes guided recipes with step-by-step instructions.

Cooking Experience

The large dome accommodates whole chickens, multiple racks of ribs, or family-sized batches with ease. Multiple cooking levels allow simultaneous preparation of different foods. Results consistently impress—crispy exteriors with perfectly tender interiors, using minimal oil.

Value for Money

At a premium price point, the Dome 2 justifies its cost through innovative features, large capacity, and time-saving automation. For busy families or cooking enthusiasts who value convenience and consistent results, it's a worthwhile investment.
