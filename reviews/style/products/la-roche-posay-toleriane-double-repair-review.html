La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair Face Moisturizer combines French pharmacy expertise with innovative prebiotic technology to deliver comprehensive barrier restoration and SPF 30 protection. Prebiotic Thermal Spring Water Technology

Sourced from the exclusive La Roche-Posay thermal spring in France, this mineral-rich water contains unique selenium and other trace elements that provide antioxidant benefits and support skin's natural microbiome balance. The prebiotic properties help maintain healthy skin flora while soothing inflammation and irritation.

Essential Ceramides & Barrier Repair

Contains ceramide-3 and essential fatty acids that restore the skin's natural protective barrier, preventing moisture loss and strengthening defense against environmental stressors. This barrier restoration technology works continuously to improve skin health and reduce sensitivity over time with consistent use.

Broad-Spectrum SPF 30 Protection

Zinc oxide and octinoxate provide reliable broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection without leaving white cast or interfering with makeup application. The mineral-chemical hybrid sunscreen approach offers effective daily protection while maintaining the moisturizer's lightweight, comfortable texture.

Sensitive Skin Formulation

Fragrance-free, paraben-free, and allergy-tested formula developed specifically for sensitive and reactive skin types. The gentle formulation has been dermatologically tested on sensitive skin and is suitable for use during compromised barrier periods or post-treatment recovery routines.

Niacinamide Skin Benefits

Includes niacinamide (Vitamin B3) to help regulate oil production, minimize pore appearance, and provide anti-inflammatory benefits. This multi-functional ingredient enhances skin texture and tone while working synergistically with ceramides and thermal water for comprehensive skin improvement.

Lightweight Daily Moisturizer

Non-comedogenic formula absorbs quickly without greasiness, making it ideal for daily use under makeup or alone. The balanced hydration provides adequate moisture for most skin types while remaining comfortable for extended wear in various weather conditions and environments.
