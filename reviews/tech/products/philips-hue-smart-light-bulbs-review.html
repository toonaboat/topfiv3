The Philips Hue A19 LED Smart Light Bulbs with White and Color Ambiance represent the gold standard in smart lighting, offering 60W-equivalent brightness with 800 lumens output and access to millions of colors. Color Range & Ambiance Control

The White and Color Ambiance range delivers both 50,000 shades of warm-to-cool white light and millions of vibrant colors. Whether you need focused daylight for productivity, warm white for relaxation, or dynamic colors for entertainment, these bulbs provide the full spectrum. The ability to instantly dim and brighten gives precise control over your lighting environment.

Flexible Connectivity Options

Built-in Bluetooth capability enables instant light control within one room for up to 10 lights without additional hardware. For expanded functionality, connecting to a Hue Bridge unlocks the complete smart lighting experience including remote control, automation, and integration with 10+ lights throughout your entire home.

Voice Control & App Integration

Seamless compatibility with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri enables effortless voice commands for lighting control. The comprehensive Philips Hue app allows detailed customization of scenes, scheduling, and automation routines. Integration with smart accessories like the Hue dimmer switch and smart button provides physical control options.

Matter Support & Future-Proofing

Built-in Matter support ensures compatibility with current and future smart home platforms, protecting your investment as technology evolves. The universal standard enables seamless operation across different ecosystems without vendor lock-in, providing long-term flexibility for smart home expansion.

Energy Efficiency & Installation

Standard E26 base fits virtually any light fixture, making installation as simple as screwing in a traditional bulb. The LED technology consumes significantly less energy than incandescent equivalents while providing 800 lumens of bright, consistent light. The bulbs are designed for long-term reliability with extended lifespans.

Scene Creation & Automation

Preset color themes provide instant ambiance transformation, while custom scene creation allows personalization for specific activities or moods. Geofencing capabilities enable automatic lighting adjustments based on presence, while scheduling ensures lights adapt to daily routines without manual intervention.
