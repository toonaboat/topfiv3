Apple AirPods Pro 2 Review
← Back to Wireless Headphones Rankings

If you're already immersed in the Apple ecosystem, the AirPods Pro 2 aren't just an upgrade—they're a meaningful leap forward. From refined design and improved audio to convenient hearing features, these earbuds deliver a surprisingly deep experience in a compact package.

Build Quality & Design

Apple has refined an iconic design. Now with USB‑C charging, built‑in Find My speaker, and an IP54-rated case, setup just makes sense. The lanyard loop and Apple's signature fit feel ultra-practical and smartphone-friendly for travel or everyday use.

Comfort & Fit

With multiple included ear tip sizes—including XS—the fit is more inclusive than ever. Many users find them secure, lightweight, and comfortable for extended sessions. Those looking for even deeper isolation may consider third-party foam tips.

Sound Quality

Powered by Apple's H2 chip, these earbuds deliver natural, detailed sound with balanced bass, clear mids, and crisp highs. Personalized Spatial Audio adds immersive depth, particularly with Dolby Atmos content. Not a hardcore bass signature, but excellent across genres.

ANC & Transparency Modes

The noise cancellation is fantastic—transformative for flights, coffee shops, or open workspaces. And the improved Transparency Mode feels natural, especially with Adaptive Audio and Conversation Awareness automatically tuning past and present noise levels.

Smart Features

With built-in hearing checks, head gesture Siri, easy multi‑device switching, and auto-device detection, the AirPods Pro 2 feel genuinely smart. These are more than just earbuds—they think along with you.

Battery & Charging

Expect around 6 hours with ANC active and up to 30 hours total with the case. USB‑C charging is speedy and flexible, with MagSafe compatibility adding wireless ease. Apple covered every base here.
