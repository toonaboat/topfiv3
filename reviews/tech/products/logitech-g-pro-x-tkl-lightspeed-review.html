The Logitech G Pro X TKL Lightspeed Gaming Keyboard represents the pinnacle of wireless gaming keyboards, delivering professional-grade performance in a compact tenkeyless design. Built for esports athletes and competitive gamers who demand wireless reliability without compromise, this keyboard combines Lightspeed wireless technology, customizable GX switches, and premium build quality for serious competitive gaming.

Wireless Performance & Connectivity

The LIGHTSPEED wireless technology delivers true 1ms report rate performance that rivals any wired connection. In extensive testing, we found zero lag, dropped inputs, or connectivity issues during competitive gaming sessions. The keyboard wakes from sleep instantly with no delay when you start typing after breaks. For versatility, Bluetooth connectivity supports up to three devices simultaneously, making it perfect for switching between gaming PC, work laptop, and mobile devices.

Build Quality & Design

Logitech hasn't compromised on build quality with this tenkeyless design. The metal backplate provides exceptional rigidity with virtually no flex, even during aggressive gaming sessions. PBT keycaps represent a significant upgrade over typical ABS plastic, resisting shine and wear while maintaining texture and legends after thousands of hours of use. The compact tenkeyless form factor removes the number pad, providing valuable desk space for mouse movement crucial for low-sensitivity FPS players.

Switch Performance & Customization

Switch customization sets this keyboard apart with options for GX Brown Tactile, GX Red Linear, or GX Blue Clicky switches. Each switch type is engineered for different gaming styles - linear for rapid-fire gaming, tactile for typing comfort, or clicky for satisfying feedback. The switches provide consistent actuation and durability rated for 70 million keystrokes.

Battery Life & Charging

Battery life exceeds expectations at up to 50 hours of continuous use with RGB lighting enabled. Even with intensive gaming sessions, you'll get multiple weeks of normal use between charges. USB-C charging is convenient and allows continued use while charging. The keyboard includes battery level indicators and power management features.

RGB Lighting & Software

LIGHTSYNC RGB technology provides per-key customization with 16.8 million colors. The lighting can display game information, highlight important keys, or create stunning visual effects. Logitech G HUB software offers extensive customization for lighting effects, key assignments, and performance settings. Unlike some gaming keyboards where RGB feels like an afterthought, here it's thoughtfully integrated.
