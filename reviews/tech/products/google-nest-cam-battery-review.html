Superior Video Quality

The 1080p HDR video at 30 FPS captures crisp, detailed footage through a 1/2.8-inch, 2-megapixel sensor with 130° diagonal field of view. The wide viewing angle ensures comprehensive coverage while 6x digital zoom allows detailed examination of specific areas. HDR technology maintains image quality across varying lighting conditions throughout the day.

Advanced Night Vision

Six infrared LED sensors provide up to 20 feet of illumination for crystal-clear night vision recording. While the system uses traditional black and white IR technology rather than newer color night vision, the image quality remains exceptionally clear for identifying people, vehicles, and activities in complete darkness.

Intelligent Recognition & Alerts

Built-in intelligence distinguishes between people, animals, and vehicles, sending targeted alerts through the Google Home app. Familiar face alerts can recognize frequent guests, allowing personalized greetings through the built-in speaker and microphone system. This smart filtering reduces false alarms while ensuring important events are never missed.

Robust Battery & Backup Storage

The built-in rechargeable 6 Ah, 3.65V lithium-ion battery provides extended operation between charges. Local storage capabilities maintain up to one hour of recorded events during Wi-Fi outages or power failures, ensuring critical footage is never lost. Three hours of free event video history provides immediate access to recent activity.

Weatherproof Durability

IP54 water resistance rating ensures reliable operation through rain, snow, and extreme weather conditions. Operating temperature range from –4°F to 104°F (–20°C to 40°C) maintains functionality in harsh climates. At 14.04 oz, the camera remains lightweight while providing rugged construction for long-term outdoor use.

Flexible Installation & Control

Wire-free design enables placement virtually anywhere without electrical constraints. Comprehensive control through the Google Home app includes live HD video streaming, recording playback, two-way communication, and system management. Integration with Google's ecosystem provides seamless smart home coordination.
