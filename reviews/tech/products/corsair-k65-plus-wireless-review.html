Perfect 75% Form Factor

Corsair's first foray into the 75% form factor proves they understand what gamers and typists actually want. The K65 Plus Wireless retains all essential keys including the F-row and arrow keys while eliminating the rarely-used number pad. At 320 x 136 x 35mm, it's compact enough for small desks while maintaining full functionality.

The layout feels natural from day one ‚Äî no learning curve or missing keys to frustrate your workflow. Whether you're gaming, coding, or general productivity, the 75% format provides the perfect balance of compactness and functionality.

This size works particularly well for dual-monitor setups where desk space is precious. You gain valuable mouse space for low-sensitivity gaming without sacrificing typing comfort or functionality.

Innovative Multi-Function Dial

The programmable multi-function dial elevates this keyboard above typical 75% competitors. Through iCUE software, you can customize the dial to control RGB brightness, system volume, zoom levels, scrolling, and countless other functions. The tactile click feedback provides satisfying confirmation of each adjustment.

The dial's versatility shines in creative workflows ‚Äî quickly scrub through video timelines, adjust brush sizes in Photoshop, or fine-tune game settings without leaving your keyboard. FN key combinations and shortcut commands provide additional customization layers for power users.

Unlike gimmicky features on some gaming keyboards, the multi-function dial proves genuinely useful in daily computing tasks. It's the kind of feature you don't realize you need until you have it.

Hot-Swappable MLX Red Switches

Hot-swappable switches represent a game-changer for keyboard customization. The included CORSAIR MLX Red switches come pre-lubricated for smooth, responsive keystrokes right out of the box. With 45g actuation force, 1.9mm actuation distance, and 4mm total travel, they strike an excellent balance for both gaming and typing.

The beauty lies in future customization. Don't like linear switches? Swap them for tactile or clicky alternatives without desoldering or buying a new keyboard. The hot-swap sockets support standard 3-pin and 5-pin MX-style switches, opening up endless customization possibilities.

Each switch is rated for 70 million keystrokes, ensuring longevity that outlasts typical keyboard lifespans. The pre-lubrication eliminates scratchiness and inconsistency that plagues cheaper mechanical switches.

Exceptional Wireless Performance & Battery

The 266-hour battery life with 2.4GHz wireless (RGB disabled) is simply exceptional. Even with RGB lighting enabled, expect multiple weeks between charges. This eliminates the battery anxiety that plagued earlier wireless keyboards.

Ultra-fast 2.4GHz wireless delivers lag-free performance identical to wired keyboards. In competitive gaming scenarios, we detected zero input lag or dropped keystrokes. The connection remains rock-solid even in crowded wireless environments.

Bluetooth connectivity supports multiple devices with AES encryption for security. Seamlessly switch between your gaming PC, work laptop, and tablet without constantly re-pairing devices. The tri-mode connectivity (wired, 2.4GHz, Bluetooth) adapts to any setup.
