Sensor Performance & Accuracy

The Focus Pro 30K sensor represents the pinnacle of optical gaming sensors, delivering flawless tracking up to 30,000 DPI with zero acceleration or smoothing. In extensive testing across multiple surfaces and gaming scenarios, we found perfect 1:1 tracking with no lift-off distance issues or spin-outs. The sensor's intelligent functions include Smart Tracking that automatically calibrates to your surface for optimal performance.

Wireless Performance & Connectivity

HyperSpeed wireless technology delivers ultra-low latency performance that's indistinguishable from wired connections. With a 1000Hz polling rate and sub-1ms click latency, competitive gamers won't notice any difference in responsiveness. The wireless connection maintains stability even in crowded RF environments, and Bluetooth connectivity provides versatility for multi-device use.

Ergonomics & Build Quality

The iconic DeathAdder shape has been refined over generations to provide exceptional comfort for extended gaming sessions. The right-handed ergonomic design fits naturally in palm and claw grip styles, with textured side grips providing secure control during intense gaming. Build quality is excellent with zero creaking or flex, despite the lightweight 82g construction.

Battery Life & Charging

Battery performance exceeds expectations with up to 90 hours of continuous use in HyperSpeed mode, or an incredible 285 hours using Bluetooth connectivity. USB-C charging is fast and convenient, with the ability to continue gaming while charging. Battery level indicators keep you informed of remaining power.

Switch Performance & Durability

Generation 3 Optical Mouse Switches provide ultra-fast actuation with zero debounce delay, delivering consistent clicks rated for 90 million actuations. The switches offer satisfying tactile feedback without being overly stiff or light. Side buttons are well-positioned and provide reliable actuation without accidental presses.
