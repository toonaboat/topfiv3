Brushless Motor Technology

The DCK299M2 features DEWALT's high efficiency brushless motors that deliver up to 57% more runtime over brushed motors. The DCD996B hammer drill delivers 3-speed performance (0-2,000 rpm) with up to 38,250 BPM for fast drilling in masonry materials. The DCF887B impact driver provides 1,825 in-lbs of max torque with speeds up to 3,250 RPM and 3,600 impacts per minute.

Professional Design Features

The hammer drill measures 8.4 inches front-to-back with lightweight 4.7 lb design, while the impact driver is 5.3 inches and weighs 3.4 lbs. The drill features a heavy-duty 1/2-inch ratcheting nitro-carburized metal chuck with carbide inserts for superior bit gripping strength. Both tools feature 3-Mode LED lighting providing illumination up to 20X brighter than previous models.

20V MAX XR Battery System

Equipped with two 20V MAX XR 4.0Ah lithium-ion battery packs (DCB204), this kit delivers extended runtime for professional applications. The DEWALT-built high power, high efficiency brushless motors deliver up to 75% more runtime compared to 18V NiCad brushed motors, ensuring maximum productivity on demanding jobsites.

Advanced Chuck and Controls

The hammer drill includes a heavy-duty 1/2-inch ratcheting chuck with carbide inserts for superior bit gripping strength and corrosion resistance. Three LED lights on both tools make them ideal for working in dark spaces, while the hammer function enables fast drilling through masonry materials when needed.

System Compatibility

Both tools are compatible with all 200+ DEWALT 20V MAX batteries and chargers, providing flexibility and expandability within the DEWALT ecosystem. The kit is designed for most drilling, hammering and fastening applications, making it suitable for demanding jobsite conditions where professional performance is required.
