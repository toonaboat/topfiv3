The CRAFTSMAN CMCK200C2AM V20 MAX Cordless Drill and Impact Driver Combo Kit delivers reliable performance for homeowners and DIY enthusiasts. This kit includes the CMCD700 drill/driver and CMCF800 impact driver, both powered by V20 MAX 20V lithium-ion technology and designed for versatility across drilling and fastening applications.

Performance & Power Output

The CMCD700 drill/driver features a motor providing 280 unit watts out (UWO) of power with a 2-speed gearbox ranging from 0-350 RPM and 0-1,500 RPM for speed of application. The CMCF800 impact driver provides 1,460 in-lbs of torque running at 2,800 RPM and 3,100 BPM, delivering the power needed for heavy duty fastening tasks.

V20 System Compatibility

A key advantage of this kit is compatibility with CRAFTSMAN's extensive V20 MAX ecosystem. The V20 system offers a comprehensive solution for all drilling and fastening needs, with the flexibility to expand into other cordless tools as project requirements grow.

Battery Performance & Charging

Equipped with two V20 MAX 1.5Ah lithium-ion batteries, the kit provides adequate runtime for typical home improvement projects. The 20V MAX battery delivers maximum initial battery voltage (measured without workload) of 20 volts, ensuring consistent power delivery throughout the discharge cycle.

Ergonomics & Design Features

Both tools feature compact designs and contoured over-molded handles that make working in tight spaces easier and more comfortable. LED lights on both units provide improved visibility in dark work areas. The impact driver features a drop and load one-handed bit change for convenient operation.

Build Quality & Construction

CRAFTSMAN delivers solid construction with attention to durability and user comfort. The tools are designed for common applications including drilling and fastening in soft and hard wood, fastening screws, lag bolts, and large fasteners. The kit includes a soft storage bag for transporting or storing the products, batteries, charger and accessories.
