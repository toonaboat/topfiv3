Makita 18V LXT Compact Combo Kit Review
← Back to Cordless Drill Combos 2025

The Makita CT225SYX 18V LXT Lithium-Ion Compact Cordless 2-Piece Combo Kit exemplifies Japanese engineering excellence in lightweight, durable tool design. This kit includes the XFD10Z 1/2" driver-drill and XDT11Z impact driver, both featuring brushed motors and designed for increased versatility across drilling and fastening applications.

Precision Engineering & Performance

The XFD10Z driver-drill features 2 speeds (0-600 and 0-1,900 RPM) and delivers up to 480 in-lbs of Max Force. The XDT11Z impact driver provides variable speed (0-2,900 RPM and 0-3,500 IPM) with 1,460 in-lbs of Max Torque. Both tools utilize brushed motors engineered for reliability and consistent performance across demanding applications.

Compact & Lightweight Design

The CT225SYX prioritizes user comfort through exceptionally compact tool dimensions. The driver-drill measures only 7-1/4 inches long and weighs 3.3 lbs with battery, while the impact driver is even more compact at 5-3/8 inches and weighs only 2.8 lbs. This compact design reduces user fatigue while enabling operation in confined spaces.

18V LXT System Integration

This kit integrates seamlessly with Makita's extensive 18V LXT system, providing compatibility with a comprehensive range of cordless tools and outdoor power equipment. The mature platform offers excellent expandability for users looking to build a complete cordless tool collection.

Battery Technology & Protection

Equipped with two 18V LXT Compact Lithium-Ion 1.5Ah batteries (BL1815N), the kit provides adequate runtime for typical applications. The batteries feature Star Protection Computer Controls to protect against overloading, over-discharging and over-heating. Extreme Protection Technology (XPT) provides increased dust and water resistance for enhanced durability.

User-Focused Features

Both tools include built-in L.E.D. lights to illuminate work areas and ergonomically designed handles with rubberized soft grips for enhanced comfort and control. The kit includes an 18V LXT Lithium-Ion Charger (DC18SD) and tool bag (831274-0) for convenient storage and transport of the complete system.
