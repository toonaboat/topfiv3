Performance & Power Delivery

The DCD771 drill/driver features a high-performance motor delivering 300 unit watts out (UWO) of power with a two-speed transmission (0-450/0-1,500 RPM). The DCF885 impact driver delivers 2,800 RPM for fast fastening applications. While these brushed motors aren't as efficient as brushless alternatives, they provide consistent performance for everyday drilling through wood, metal, and light masonry work.

Battery Life & Charging

Equipped with two compact 1.3Ah 20V MAX lithium-ion batteries, this kit provides adequate runtime for light to moderate DIY projects. The batteries are compatible with DEWALT's FLEXVOLT system for extended runtime when needed. The included charger efficiently recharges depleted batteries, keeping you productive throughout your projects.

Design & Ergonomics

Both tools feature DEWALT's signature compact and lightweight design to fit into tight areas. The DCD771 includes a 1/2-inch single-sleeve ratcheting chuck providing tight bit gripping strength, while the DCF885 impact driver measures just 5.55 inches front-to-back and weighs only 2.8 lbs. The impact driver features a 3-LED light ring with 20-second delay for superior visibility without shadows.

Versatility & Applications

This combo excels at a wide range of applications including drilling, fastening, and light construction work. The drill's 1/2-inch single sleeve ratcheting chuck provides secure bit retention, while the impact driver's one-handed loading 1/4-inch hex chuck accepts 1-inch bit tips. Both tools are designed for versatility and portability across various job sites.
